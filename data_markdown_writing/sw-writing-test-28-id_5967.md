## 2. Respond to a written request

**Request 1**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
From: Eagle Fitness Center
To: Glendale Apartment Residents
Subject: Relocation of Eagle Fitness Center
Sent: May 3, 01:20 P.M.
Dear residents,
We are scheduled to relocate our fitness center closer to your apartment complex and hope to provide better service to you. If you have any good ideas about how we can improve our facilities, please let us know. We will try to apply them to our fitness center.
Thank you,
Eagle Fitness Center
Directions: Respond to the e-mail as if you are a resident in the Glendale apartment complex. In your e-mail, give TWO suggestions regarding the facilities and ask one question.
```

**Request 2**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
From: Alpha Tours
To: Advertising List Subject: Package Family Tour
Sent: July 3, 12:45 P.M
Alpha Tours is offering a family package to Bali for this upcoming summer vacation. Our package includes hotel accommodation, airfare, and much more. For more information, please contact us via email at touragent@joy.com
Directions: Respond to the e-mail as if you are a customer that is interested in the package. In your e-mail, ask for three pieces of information about the package tour.
```
