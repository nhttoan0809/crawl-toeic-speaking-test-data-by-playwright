**Request 1**

```text

To: Slick Shoes Customer Support
Subject: Wrong Order Received
Dear Customer Support,
I recently ordered a pair of black, high-heeled shoes from your website. However, I received a pair of work boots instead. Could you help me get the correct order?
Thank you,
Jennifer Watson
Directions: Respond to the e-mail, as if you are a representative at the customer support. In your e-mail, give ONE piece of information and make TWO requests.
```
