## 2. Respond to a written request

**Request 1**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
From: Luis
To: Michelle
Subject: Hotel advice
Sent: February 10, 11:31 P.M.
Hi, Michelle.
How have you been? I hope you are fine.
I am scheduled to go on a business trip next month, and I will stay at the SKY Hotel. I heard that you stayed there last time. How was the hotel?
If you could give me some information on the hotel, I would really appreciate it.
Regards, Lui
Directions: Respond to the e-mail. In your e-mail, include THREE inconvenient details.
```

**Request 2**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
From: Jeff
To: SKY Institute
Subject: Part-time job
Sent: May 20, 09:31 Α.Μ.
To whom it may concern:
I saw your job opening on the AVD Web site, and I am interested in the part-time job.

Jeff
Directions: Respond to the e-mail. In your e-mail, give TWO pieces of information about the SKY Institute and ONE suggestion for applying for the part-time job.
```
