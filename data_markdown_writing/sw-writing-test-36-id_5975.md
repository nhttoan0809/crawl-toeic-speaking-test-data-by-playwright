## 2. Respond to a written request

**Request 1**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
From: Michael Nguyen
To: Sarah Lee
Subject: Car Repair Shop Recommendation
Hi Sarah,
I remember you mentioned getting your car fixed recently. I’m looking for a good car repair shop, and I was wondering if you could recommend the one you used. Could you give me some details about it?
Thanks,
Michael
Directions: Respond to the e-mail as if you are Sarah Lee. In your e-mail, give THREE pieces of information.
```

**Request 2**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
From: John Dembo, TMR Industries
To: Susan Hoffman, TMR Industries
Subject: Employee Awards Dinner
Hi Susan,
I’m coordinating the employee awards dinner this year and know you’ve handled this in the past. Could you tell me about your experience planning previous dinners? Also, I’d appreciate any suggestions you might have.
Thanks,
John
Directions: Respond to the e-mail. In your e-mail, give one piece of information and make two suggestions.
```
