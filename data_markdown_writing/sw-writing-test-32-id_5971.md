## 2. Respond to a written request

**Request 1**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
From: Technical support team
To: All employees
Subject: Internet Access Issues
Hello everyone,
We’ve been informed that some of you are having trouble accessing the Internet on company computers. To help us fix this issue, please let us know if you’ve encountered such problems. Share a detailed description of the problem and the steps you took before it occurred.
Thank you,
Technical Support Team
Jennifer White
Directions: Directions: Respond to the e-mail as if you are a staff member in the company. In your e-mail, give TWO pieces of information and ask ONE question.
```

**Request 2**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
From: Kathryn Fogle
To: Joey Longwood
Subject: End of Training Cycle Staff Party

Thank you,
Kathryn
Directions: Respond to the e-mail as if you are Joey Longwood. In your e-mail, make TWO suggestions and ask ONE question.
```
