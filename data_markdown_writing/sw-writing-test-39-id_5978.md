## 2. Respond to a written request

**Request 1**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
From: Jennifer Adams
To: Customer Service
Subject: Paint Availability
Hello,
I’m planning to repaint my living room this weekend, and I’d like to know if you carry the “Fresh White” paint from the Bright

Home brand. Also, do you offer delivery for paint orders?
Thank you,
Jennifer
Directions: Respond to the e-mail as if you work at the home improvement store. In your e-mail, provide TWO pieces of information and ask ONE question.
```

**Request 2**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
From: Isabella Martin
To: Office Manager
Subject: Request for Office Supplies
Dear Office Manager,
I hope this email finds you well. I have recently run out of several office supplies and would appreciate it if you could help me with restocking. Could you please let me know which supplies are currently available and if there are any new items we should be aware of?
Thank you for your assistance.
Best regards,
Isabella Martin
Directions: Respond to the e-mail as if you are the Office Manager. In your e-mail, give TWO pieces of information and make ONE request.
```
