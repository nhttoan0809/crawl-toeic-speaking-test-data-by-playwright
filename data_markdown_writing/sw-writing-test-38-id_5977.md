## 2. Respond to a written request

**Request 1**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
From: Beth Stewart
To: EZ Used Cars
Subject: Ordering Process
Dear Sir/Madam,
I have been browsing your site and found a vehicle that I’m interested in. I was wondering about the ordering process but couldn’t find the information I needed on your site. Could you please tell me the best way to get more details on how to order?
Thank you,
Beth Stewart
Directions: Respond to the e-mail as if you are a staff member in the used car company. In your e-mail, give ONE piece of information and make TWO requests.
```

**Request 2**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
From: David Kim
To: Customer Support
Subject: Trouble Logging In
Hello,
I’ve been trying to log in to my account for the past two days, but the system keeps saying my password is incorrect. I already tried resetting it, but I still can’t access my account. Could you please help me with this?
Best regards,
David
Directions: Respond to the e-mail as if you work in the customer service department of an online learning platform. In your e-mail, make TWO suggestions and ask ONE question.
```
