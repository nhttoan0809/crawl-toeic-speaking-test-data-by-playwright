## 2. Respond to a written request

**Request 1**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
From: Daniel Adams
To: IT Support Team
Subject: Urgent: Software Issue
Dear IT Support Team,
I’m having an issue with the accounting software on my computer. It crashes a few seconds after I try to open it, which is delaying my work. Could you please tell me what steps I should take to fix this, or if you need to access my computer remotely to solve the problem?
Thank you for your prompt attention to this matter.
Best regards,
Daniel Adams
Directions: Respond to the e-mail as if you are a member of the IT Support Team. In your e-mail, provide TWO pieces of information and make ONE suggestion.
```

**Request 2**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
From: Sarah White
To: Human Resources Department
Subject: Annual Leave Request
Dear Human Resources Team,
I am writing to request annual leave from August 15th to August 22nd. I have checked with my manager, and they have approved the dates. Could you please confirm if there are any forms I need to fill out or procedures I need to follow to finalize this request?
Thank you for your assistance.
Best regards,
Sarah White
Directions: Respond to the e-mail as if you are a member of the Human Resources Department. In your e-mail, provide TWO pieces of information and make ONE request.
```
