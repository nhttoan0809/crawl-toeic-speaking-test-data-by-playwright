## 2. Respond to a written request

**Request 1**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
From: Karen Smith
To: Art Club Members
Subject: Suggestions for Our Art Club
Hello everyone,
As we plan for the upcoming year, we would love to hear your suggestions on how we can make our art club even better. What activities or events would you like to see us organize? Please share your ideas.
Best,
Karen
Directions: Respond to the e-mail as if you are a member of the art club. In your e-mail, give THREE suggestions.
```

**Request 2**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
From: Belinda Cho
To: Tomas Cardenas
Subject: Client dinner on Friday
Tomas,
Can you please find and book a suitable restaurant for my meeting with the representatives from Tanaka Corporation? We will go directly to the restaurant after our afternoon meeting, which should finish by 4 P.M.
Thanks,
Belinda
Directions: Respond to the e-mail as if you are Tomas Cardenas, Ms. Cho's personal assistant. In your e-mail, provide THREE pieces of information.
```
