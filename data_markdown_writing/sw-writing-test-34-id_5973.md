## 2. Respond to a written request

**Request 1**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
From: Meredith Scott
To: Aura Catering
Subject: Party Catering Services
Hello,
I’m planning a party for my son’s graduation in a couple of weeks and am looking for a caterer. Could you tell me more about the services you offer?
Thanks,
Meredith
Directions: Respond to the e-mail as if you are a staff member in the catering service. In your e-mail, give ONE piece of information and make TWO requests.
```

**Request 2**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
From: The Hive
To: Serena Blake
Subject: We Value Your Feedback
Hello,
We’re looking to improve our restaurant and would love to hear from you. Could you please share some feedback from your last visit to The Hive? We’d like to know your thoughts on the service, food, and overall experience. Any suggestions would be greatly appreciated.
Thank you for your time.
Directions: Respond to the e-mail as if you are a customer of the restaurant. In your e-mail, give TWO pieces of information and make ONE suggestion.
```
