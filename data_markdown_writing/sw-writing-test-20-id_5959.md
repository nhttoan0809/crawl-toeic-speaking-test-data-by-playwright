## 2. Respond to a written request

**Request 1**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
FROM: EZ Travel, Inc.
TO: Karl Ionesco
SUBJECT: Feedback follow-up
SENT: July 20, 1:16 P.M.
Dear Mr. Ionesco,
A few days ago, you completed our survey and indicated dissatisfaction with our flight- and hotel-booking services. Please provide details about the problems you experienced so that we can improve our travel services.
Thank you.
Karin
EZ Travel, Inc.
Directions: Respond to the e-mail. In your e-mail, give THREE details.
```

**Request 2**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
FROM: Daniel Zimmerman
TO: Susan Kahn
SUBJECT: Workshops
SENT: January 18, 3:47 P.M.
Dear Ms. Kahn,
A friend recommended your workshops. I think my colleagues could benefit from more professional development opportunities. Could you tell me about the various workshops you offer?
Thanks,
Daniel
Directions: Respond to the e-mail. In your e-mail, give TWO pieces of information and ask ONE question.
```
