## 2. Respond to a written request

**Request 1**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
From: Subaki Tanasha, Sales Manager Assistant
To: Suzy Wang, Sales Manager
Subject: Sales Associate Job Posting
Hi Suzy,
I’ve been assigned to post the job opening for the Sales Associate position on our company’s website. To make sure we attract the right candidates, could you provide me with some details about the specific qualities and qualifications you’re looking for?
Thank you for your guidance.
Best regards,
Subaki Tanasha
Directions: Respond to the e-mail as if you are Suzy Wang, the Sales Manager. In your e-mail, provide THREE pieces of information.
```

**Request 2**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
From: Technical Support Team
To: All Employees
Subject: Internet Access Issues
Hello everyone,
We’ve heard that some of you are having trouble with the Internet on company computers. To fix this issue, please let us know if you’ve experienced any problems. Describe what happened and what steps you took before the issue occurred.
Thank you,
Technical Support Team
Jennifer White
Directions: Respond to the e-mail as if you are a staff member in the company. In your e-mail, give TWO pieces of information and ask ONE question.
```
