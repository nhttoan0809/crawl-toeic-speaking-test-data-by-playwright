## 2. Respond to a written request

**Request 1**

```text
Respond to an email
From: Ken Smith
To: Aaron Devlin, President, First Insurance Agency
Subject: Interview .
Sent: March 29, 8:16 A.M.
Dear Mr. Devlin,
I have a job interview scheduled for next Monday at 3:00 P.M. However, I am afraid there have been some changes in my current schedule, so I have to work until late at night that day. Could you possibly reschedule my interview date? I look forward to meeting you soon.
Best regards,
Ken Smith
Directions: Respond to the e-mail as if you are Aaron Devlin. In your e-mail, suggest TWO possible times for holding the interview and request ONE item that Ken Smith should bring with him.
```
