## 2. Respond to a written request

**Request 1**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
From: Raymond Steele
To: Wentworth Community Center
Subject: Information about Volunteer Program
Dear Wentworth Community Center,
I found out from your website that you have a volunteer program for kids. I’m looking for a program for my four-year-old daughter, and yours seems like something she might enjoy. Could you provide me with more details about the program?
Thank you,
Raymond
Directions: Respond to the e-mail as if you are an employee from the Wentworth Community Center. In your e-mail, provide TWO pieces of information and ask ONE question.
```

**Request 2**

```text
Direction: Read the e-mail below.
From: Paul Franco
To: Teala Sandars
Subject: Your Real Estate Brochure Designer
Dear Teala,
I want to create a great brochure for a client. I was impressed by the real estate brochure you used and was wondering if you could tell me which company made it for you, if it’s not too much trouble.
Thanks,
Paul
Directions: Respond to the e-mail as if you are Teala Sandars. In your e-mail, give THREE pieces of information.
```
